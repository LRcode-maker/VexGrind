VEX V5 Robotics

Complete Parts Inventory Guide

Everything you need for building, competing, and learning with VEX V5

Electronics Structure Motion Pneumatics

Electronics (V5 Control System)

V5 Robot Brain
Central processing unit with touchscreen, 8 smart ports, and 3-wire ports. Powers the entire robot.
Core Component
V5 Smart Motor
High-performance motor with built-in encoder, current/temperature sensors, and gear cartridge options (11W, 18W, or high-speed).
Motion + Feedback
V5 Controller (Joystick)
Wireless controller with LCD screen, buttons, joysticks, and battery. Supports driver and partner controllers.
V5 Battery (Li-ion)
Rechargeable 12.8V lithium-ion battery with smart power management.
V5 Inertial Sensor
Measures acceleration, rotation (gyro), and orientation for advanced autonomous control.
V5 Distance Sensor
Measures distance to objects using ultrasonic or laser technology.
V5 Optical Sensor
Detects color, hue, proximity, and ambient light.
V5 Vision Sensor
Camera-based sensor for object detection, color tracking, and AI-like vision tasks.
V5 Rotational Sensor
High-resolution encoder for measuring shaft rotation (e.g., for odometry or arms).
V5 AI Vision Sensor
Advanced vision sensor with object recognition capabilities.

Structure (Metal & Plates)

C-Channel
Main structural beams in various lengths (e.g., 2x2x20, 1x2x10). Provides strength and mounting points.
U-Channel / Angle
Lighter structural pieces for frames, supports, and custom shapes.
Plates (Flat & Bent)
Solid metal plates for bases, gussets, and reinforcements.
Bars & Rails
Linear structural elements, often used for linear motion or sliders.
Gussets & Brackets
Corner reinforcements and connectors for stronger joints.
PET / Acrylic Sheets
Non-metal panels for custom body panels or lightweight structures.

Motion Components

Wheels (Omni, Traction, Mecanum, etc.)
Various diameters and types for different drive systems (tank, holonomic, etc.).
Gears (12T, 36T, 60T, etc.)
For speed/torque adjustments and mechanical advantage.
Sprockets & Chain
For non-slip power transmission over distance.
Axles (High Strength & Regular)
Shafts for transmitting rotation (1/4" square high-strength axles recommended for V5 motors).
Bearings & Pillow Blocks
Reduce friction and support rotating shafts.
Gear Cartridges (for Smart Motors)
Change motor output speed/torque (Red, Green, Blue cartridges).

Pneumatics

Pneumatic Cylinder
Linear actuators powered by compressed air for lifting, grabbing, or punching.
Air Tank / Reservoir
Stores compressed air.
Solenoid Valve
Electronically controls air flow to cylinders.
Compressor
Generates compressed air (usually powered by a V5 motor).
Tubing & Fittings
Hoses and connectors for the pneumatic system.
